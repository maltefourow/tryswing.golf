How it works

From box to data in under a minute.

No setup. No calibration jig. No learning curve.

Clip on

Open the split-shell sensor, click it around your shaft at the ferrule position. Snap the grip sensor onto the butt end. Done.

Calibrate

Set the club flat on the ground, face square. Tap one button in the app. The sensor reads gravity, learns its orientation, and locks in. Five seconds.

Hit balls

The sensors detect every swing automatically. In batch mode, data uploads every five swings. In live mode, you see each swing instantly.

See your swing

Open the app. Your swing data is there — speed, path, face angle, consistency scores. Session over session, the trends tell the story.
